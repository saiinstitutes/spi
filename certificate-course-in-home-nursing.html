Certificate in Home Nursing

In a home nursing course, students will learn about the principles and practices of nursing care, including how to assess patient needs, administer medications and treatments, and provide basic medical care. They may also learn about the role of a home nurse in coordinating care with other healthcare professionals, as well as how to communicate with patients and their families.

In addition to learning about nursing care, students in a home nursing course may also learn about the legal and ethical considerations involved in providing care in the home setting. Some courses may also cover topics such as home safety, patient education, and managing patient care in the home environment.

Duration of the Course:

6 months

Examination:

The student will be permitted to appear in the examination only after filing the examination appearance form duly forwarded to the principal. He / She will be allowed to make three attempt for qualifying the same according to the session whether the student has filled examination appearance form or not, thereafter the admission will be cancelled and student has to take fresh admission.

  • Minimum 40% marks are required to pass in theory. Students failing in the theory paper have to appear in the supplementary examination.

  • The decision or result prepared and declared by the examining body of the institute will be final and binding on the student.
